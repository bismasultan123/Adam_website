Health Focus

Why Air Duct & Dryer Vent Cleaning?

Your air ducts act as the respiratory system of your home. Over time, they accumulate dust, pet dander, and mold spores that circulate every time the air turns on.

  • Healthier Air: Removes dust, allergens, pet dander, and lint to improve indoor air quality.
  • Fire Safety: Clears flammable lint and debris, helping reduce fire risks in your home.
  • Energy Efficiency Improves airflow so systems run better, dry faster, and use less energy.
Safety First

Why Chimney Cleaning?

Every time you enjoy a fire, a sticky substance called Creosote builds up in your flue. This material is the #1 cause of residential structure fires.

  • Fire Prevention: Removes flammable tar before it ignites.
  • CO Safety: Ensures carbon monoxide vents out, not into your home.
  • Longevity: Protects your masonry from expensive soot damage.
